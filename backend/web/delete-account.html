Account & Data Deletion Request (POSBah)

Last Updated: June 17, 2026

POSBah respects your privacy and gives you full control over your personal and business data. If you decide to stop using our services, you can request the permanent deletion of your account and all associated records.

Warning: Account deletion is permanent and irreversible. Once deleted, all transaction history, invoices, product lists, and store data will be deleted forever.

1. How to Request Deletion

You can request account and data deletion through either of the following methods:

  • Method A (Email Request): Send an email from your registered account email address to muhammadmuizz8@gmail.com with the subject line "Request for Account Deletion".
  • Method B (In-App Request): Navigate to Settings > Account Profile > tap Request Account Deletion. Your request will be securely transmitted to our administrators.

2. Data Types That Will Be Deleted

Upon verification of your request, the following information will be permanently purged from our server database:

  • Account Information: Your name, email address, password hashes, and profile picture.
  • Store & Transaction Ledger: All transaction records (POS, F&B, Rental, Laundry), sales reports, and client directories.
  • Business Catalog: Product names, pricing, categorizations, and stock inventories.
  • Employee Records: Karyawan logs, PINs, and attendance databases.
  • Media Assets: Digital receiver signatures and uploaded invoice images.

3. Data Retention and Processing Period

  • Processing Time: Deletion requests are processed and verified within 7 days of submission.
  • Retention Policy: We do not retain any backups or archives of your personal or business database once deletion is complete.

4. Contact for Inquiries

For any questions or urgent data removal requests, please reach out directly to our support desk: muhammadmuizz8@gmail.com
